<Programs>

Rosetta Institute Neurobiology Program

My senior year, I attended the Rosetta Insitute's Summer program in Neurobiology at UCSD, where I attended college level lectures, developed presentations, and did hands-on laboratory experiments. I learned about how normal neurologic processes are disrupted by disease. I also learned how understanding these pathways can be the key to implementing precision medicine.

Stanford Pre-Collegiate Entrepreneurship Program

My junior year of high school, I attended the Stanford Pre-Collegiate Business and Entrepreneurship program, which taught business skils such as how to write a business plan, how to use spreadsheets, and developing effective presentations. I was awarded by the professor the following honors: Best Final Pitch and Best Business Plan.

Kode with Klossy Program

Also my senior year of highschool, I attended the Kode with Klossy's program, a two week coding bootcamp, that introduced me to some new coding skills. I really enjoyed learning more coding skills and working with my partner to create a website designed for girls to help them find coding opportunities including clubs, summer camps and other educational opportunities. I found the "girls only" environment very encouraging and empowering, making me want to create this enviornment for girls wherever I go and inspired me to create this website!
